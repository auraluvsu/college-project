About This Course

Embark on an exciting journey into the fundamental building blocks of our universe with our A-Level Chemistry course. This intellectually stimulating programme will equip you with a deep understanding of chemical principles, reactions, and their real-world applications.

  • Physical Chemistry: Uncover the underlying principles governing chemical reactions, including:
    • Thermodynamics
    • Kinetics
    • Equilibrium.

  • Inorganic Chemistry: Explore the properties and behaviour of elements and their compounds, including:
    • Transition Metals
    • Periodicity

  • Organic Chemistry: Investigate the structure, bonding, reactions, and synthesis of carbon-based compounds, the foundation of life.
  • Analytical Chemistry: Develop essential skills in identifying and quantifying substances through various techniques.

Why study A-Level Chemistry at RUTC?

  • Expert tuition from experienced and passionate chemistry specialists.
  • Access to state-of-the-art laboratories.
  • Excellent preparation for university and future careers.

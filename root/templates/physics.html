About This Course

Explore the fundamental laws governing our universe with our A-Level Physics course. This challenging and insightful programme develops your understanding of the physical world, from the subatomic to the cosmic.

You'll investigate core areas such as:

  • Mechanics: Forces, Motion, Energy
  • Electricity: Circuits, fields and electromagnetism
  • Waves: Light, sound and behaviour of matter
  • Materials: Properties & behaviour of matter

This course builds strong problem-solving, analytical, and practical skills. Our dedicated tutors will guide you through experiments and complex theories, fostering a deep scientific understanding.
Ideal for those with a strong aptitude for science and maths, A-Level Physics is excellent preparation for university degrees in physics, engineering, computer science, and other scientific fields, leading to diverse and exciting careers.

Why study A-Level Physics at RUTC?

  • Expert tuition from experienced and passionate Physicists.
  • Hands on practical experience in well-equiped facilities.
  • Excellent preparation for university and future careers.
